APPLICATION PROCESS

Dubai Visa Application Process

Residence permit is where every applicant should start from.

Only after acquiring the residence permit, can you should apply for a work permit.

You residence permit will be used for authentication of all purchases made by you. Plus, you’ll have to show your residence permit when renting a house in Dubai.

If you’re applying for a work permit, then you’ll need a job.

Your Dubai based employer will be responsible for nominating you.

Once you are nominated, you need to file necessary paperwork for immigration.

A health check-up is necessary for getting any permit to the UAE.

After getting your health certificate and job nomination, you can apply for a work permit.

Once you do that, you’ll need to obtain a residency permit.

Further, you’ll have to show evidence that you have required qualification and work experience for the job you applied.

Lastly, you’ll need a valid passport which will not expire for at least the next 6 months.
