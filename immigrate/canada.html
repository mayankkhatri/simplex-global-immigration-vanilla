What are the pathways to get PR in Canada from India?

Below are five sought after ways to immigrate to Canada as a permanent resident. They are described as follows:

  1. Express Entry Program - Launched in 2015, Express Entry is an online application management system that governs all federal streams under economic migration programs, which includes Federal Skilled Worker, Canadian Experience Class, and Federal Skilled Trades. Under this system, people who wish to live and work in Canada, and who meet the minimum eligibility requirements may submit an expression of interest in applying for permanent residence by creating an online profile.

    Every profile under the Express Entry is given a Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score that is further used to rank applicants against each other in the pool. The higher the scores, the better are the chances for receiving an Invitation to Apply from Immigration, Refugees, and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).

  2. Canada Provincial Nominee Programs - The Canadian government has given its provinces rights in selecting economic immigrants and inviting them to permanently settle in Canada. Every province in Canada has its own levels of guidelines and selection criteria to pick candidates to address labor shortage needs of that particular province. All these provincial immigration streams are popularly known as Provincial Nominee Programs (PNPs).
  3. Quebec Skilled Worker Program - As compared to any other Canadian province, Quebec enjoys a high degree of authority to set its own rules for immigration. Presently, no Quebec immigration stream is aligned with Express Entry. However, candidates may choose to apply directly for the Quebec Skilled Worker Program, the Quebec Experience Class, and Quebec Business Immigration.
  4. Student Pathway- Student pathway is a definitive choice for any one who is not eligible for any of the immigration streams mentioned above. Interested candidates may choose to study in Canada as the credentials they gain while studying here can instantly improve their chances for obtaining Canada PR visa. Since many immigration programs give preference to Canadian student experience, studying in Canada can be an ideal way to get a Canadian permanent resident status.
  5. Start-up Visa-This visa category is suitable for high net-worth entrepreneurs who want to set up a new business in Canada. For this, they must get their business or business idea sponsored by an authorized body in Canada and thus migrate to the country permanently.
  6. Family Sponsorship- Family reunification is the second most popular way of migrating to Canada permanently. If you are married to person or have a child who is a Canadian citizen, they can sponsor you to come to Canada. The only condition is that the sponsor must be financially strong enough to support you in Canada.

Canada Express Entry

Migration to Canada is now an easy affair, and with the Canada Express Entry system you can live and work in Canada for a long period of time. Every skilled professional looking for lucrative job opportunities applies for permanent residency under one of the federal economic immigration programs namely Federal Skilled Worker Program, Federal Skilled Trades Program, and Canada Experience Class.

Launched in 2015, the Canada express entry is basically an online immigration system which manages skilled workers applications for permanent residence to Canada. It is designed as a competitive immigration system that ranks all the qualified applicants against one another and then sending them an Invitation to Apply for securing a permanent residence Canada status.

What is Express Entry system?

Canada express entry or EEP can be defined as a fast track immigration pathway to gain permanent residency for skilled professionals. Under this system applicants are ranked against one another based on the selection factors of age, work experience, educational qualifications, proficiency in English or French language, as well as additional factors like arranged employment and a genuine intention to live and economically establish themselves in any one of the Canadian provinces.

How does Canada Immigration Express Entry Work?

  1. STEP 1 - This involves you to fill out an online express entry form with details of nationality, age, language ability, family members, education qualifications, work experience as well as details on any job offer.
  2. STEP 2 - Just keep all your documents like language tests ready to ensure that you are eligible for Canada Express entry. Once the application is filed in the pool the applicant is issued an Express Entry Profile number and Job Seeker Validation code. Using both, the candidate can build their resume their resume in the Canada Job bank.
  3. STEP 3 - After doing all the formalities you will be ranked in the Express Entry pool using the points-based system. This score will be based on the information provided by you in the profile.
  4. STEP 4 - The highest scoring candidates are send an Invitation to apply for permanent residence. If send an ITA, you will have 90 days within which you will be required to submit your application.

Canada Express Entry Eligibility

For you to be eligible to apply for express entry you will be required to score a minimum of 67 Canada express entry points based on the factors of age, education qualifications, work experience, whether you have a valid job offer, English and/or French language skills as well as adaptability how well you’re likely to settle here. These scores are out of 100 based on the point’s assessment grid.

Your points will be granted based on how well you fare based on these six selection factors to assess eligibility of any of the three chief economic federal programs.
