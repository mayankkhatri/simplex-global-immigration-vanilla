Conditions for Non-Refund

In case the visa application faces rejection or encounters issues under the following circumstances, please note that Simplex Global Immigration Services LLP will not provide any refund or compensation:

  1. There is no refund in case of visa rejection or file goes on hold.
  2. The client fails to cooperate by providing all required documents for filing the case with the Visa Office within 7 days after signing the Contract of Engagement for our services.
  3. No refund shall be granted on withdrawal cases initiated by the client after the initial 7-day period from the process commencement date.
  4. No refund if the file is placed on hold or gets rejected by immigration authorities. Simplex Global Immigration is not liable for delays caused by Government Authorities or Foreign Embassies.
  5. The case is rejected, or the visa is revoked due to the client providing false information, misrepresentation, engaging in fraud, failing to adhere to the Agreement's terms and conditions, or withholding relevant information during application processing or the Visa Office interview.
  6. The client fails to respond or coordinate adequately with their assigned case manager via phone or email regarding their application process, despite receiving multiple reminders. In such instances, the case will be closed automatically without the possibility of reopening, and no refund request will be considered.
  7. The client fails to attend the scheduled visa interview or does not pass the visa interview.
  8. The client or their family members fail to provide required medical examination results or documentation.
  9. No compensation is provided if the client fails to meet the qualification or educational credential requirements set forth by the relevant immigration authorities.
  10. In the event of visa rejection or application refusal by the embassy/consulate, the client's file will be closed by Simplex Global Immigration without prior notification. Simplex will not be liable for any refund or further services towards the client in this scenario.
  11. Simplex Global Immigration bears no responsibility if the client submits fraudulent documents, as the entire immigration process relies on legal and authentic submissions.
  12. Simplex Global Immigration is not responsible for providing a refund if the client withdraws their case due to personal reasons, unforeseen circumstances, or any reason not attributable to Simplex, as the process involves government procedures and legal affairs.
